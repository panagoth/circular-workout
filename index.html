4-Day Circular Workout Program

Designed for Beginners

Day 1 & 3: Upper Body & Core

  • Dumbbell Chest Press: 10-12 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 10-12 reps
  • Plank: 20-30 seconds

Day 2 & 4: Lower Body & Core

  • Glute Bridges: 12-15 reps
  • Low Step-Ups: 10 reps each leg
  • Side Lunges: 10 reps each leg
